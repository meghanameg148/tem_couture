Yay or Nay: Clout goggles edition

One of our most recent new change of accessory are glasses. Gone are the days of simple Ray-Bans with black frames, and now sun glasses have been created as a use for personal statement for yourself.

Clout goggles, though famously memed and created for the Soundcloud rapper aesthetic are now popular among many famous faces.Moreover, due to the revive of the 80s style and Kurt Cobain.Furthermore, their unusual elliptical rims seem to help suit a wide range of face shapes and now, many online stores are creating more versions, creating different patterns such as checkers and in more colours such as black,red and neon green.

Though these glasses are associated with many negative archetypes of the typical teenager, we approve these goggles when using them for a laugh, but NEVER use these for actual style!
